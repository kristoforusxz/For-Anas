Our First Date

Do you remember our first date — the moment I picked you up at your place? While waiting for you to get in the car, I was trying to find the perfect song for us to listen to, something to set the mood. At the same time, I was trying to reverse the car, distracted between the playlist and the wheel… and well, I ended up crashing into a Volkswagen Scirocco. As embarrassing as that was, what stuck with me wasn’t the dent — it was you. Throughout the date, you subtly comforted me without making me feel worse. That quiet kindness, the way you understood without needing words — it made me like you even more.
